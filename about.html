My Name is P-WILSON UCHENNA. The "P" in my Name stands for "PERSISTENT". I won't stop chasing my dreams for anything. You can call me Persistent Wilson Uchenna.
Few years ago, I dealt with low self Esteem. I had nothing to show for my existence; I was just breathing. i couldn't spell or write words. I couldn't even speak good English as of then.
But one day, miracles happened. On one occasion during my moments of sober reflection. I asked myself and I quote: "why did you deal with low self esteem?" I searched for the answers and realized that I was part of the people poisoning my mind. I affirmed wrongly and always believed the evil words people threw at me.
Today, in the new world era, I have decide to become a Life Confidence Coach(I'm certified form @transformational Academy). I will help people build their confidence and unleash their potentials. I'm currently on my way to becoming a certified Cognitive Behaviorial Therapist. Cognitive behavioral therapy is a psycho-social intervention that aims to reduce symptom of various mental health conditions, primarily depression and anxiety disorders.

I will help you delete the evil you say about yourself and replace it with beautiful words for healthy living.
